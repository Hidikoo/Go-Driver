Photini's Go-Driver

Next-Generation Advocacy & Compliance Platform

Author: Heidi Kalske-Akintobi — Human rights advocate using AI and open source for social change.

Overview

Photini's Go-Driver is a modular, privacy-first toolkit for social advocacy, legal aid, and emergency field teams—designed for NGOs, legal professionals, and grassroots projects. Powered by AI, designed for compliance, and architected for real-world impact in Europe and beyond.

System Architecture

Key Features

  • Case Management Dashboard: Secure, checklist-driven, role-based workflow
  • Evidence Processing: AI-powered OCR, instant translation, trauma-aware intake
  • Legal & Compliance Tracker: Immutable audits, deadline alerts, policy rules
  • Partner Network & Collaboration: Real-time sharing, cross-org chat, file exchange, consent logging
  • Mobile App: Offline, trauma-informed, AR evidence coaching, SOS/service finder
  • Advanced AI: Risk, trauma and policy detection, multi-language support, smart routing
