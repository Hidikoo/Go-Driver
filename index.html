What is Go-Driver?

Go-Driver is a modular, privacy-first toolkit for social advocacy, legal aid, and emergency field support. It is an AI-powered co-pilot for human rights advocates—transforming scattered evidence, manual workflows, and compliance friction into a secure, auditable system where humans stay in control.

It consolidates case files, maintains immutable audit trails, provides AI recommendations (with mandatory human review), and embeds trauma-sensitive language analysis—all while meeting GDPR/EU AI Act requirements from the ground up.

Built for NGOs, lawyers, and grassroots defenders who protect vulnerable populations—especially children and asylum seekers.

Core Philosophy:

"Misinformation is more dangerous than responsible data sharing. We build systems that protect truth, privacy, and human dignity—with AI as a partner, not a replacement."

The Problem We Solve

📂

Evidence Fragmentation

Case files scattered across devices, emails, platforms—losing critical documentation when it matters most.

🔍

Audit Trail Gaps

No accountability chain for who accessed what, when, and why—compromising case integrity.

🤖

AI Black Boxes

AI tools making decisions affecting vulnerable people without human oversight or explainability.

⚖️

Compliance Friction

Privacy regulations treated as afterthought, not embedded in system architecture from day one.

Our Solution: Compliance-by-Design

Instead of bolting compliance onto existing systems, Go-Driver embeds ethics into the database schema, API logic, and AI workflows from architecture up. This means:

  • ✅ Immutable audit trails automatically maintained at database layer
  • ✅ Mandatory human review for high-risk AI decisions
  • ✅ Privacy controls enforced before application logic runs
  • ✅ Trauma-informed language scoring built into NLP systems
  • ✅ GDPR/EU AI Act compliance integrated from ground zero
