What is Go-Driver?

Go-Driver is a modular, privacy-first toolkit for social advocacy, legal aid, and emergency field support. It is an AI-powered co-pilot for human rights advocates‚Äîtransforming scattered evidence, manual workflows, and compliance friction into a secure, auditable system where humans stay in control.

It consolidates case files, maintains immutable audit trails, provides AI recommendations (with mandatory human review), and embeds trauma-sensitive language analysis‚Äîall while meeting GDPR/EU AI Act requirements from the ground up.

Built for NGOs, lawyers, and grassroots defenders protecting vulnerable populations. Engineered for full EU AI Act & GDPR compliance from day one.
